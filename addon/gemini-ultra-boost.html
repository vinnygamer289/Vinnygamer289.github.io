What is Gemini Ultra Boost?

Gemini Ultra Boost is a lag-reducing resource pack designed for Minecraft Bedrock Edition (PE). Created to enhance performance for low or mid-range devices, allowing you to have a smooth experience without worrying about FPS drops.

Key Features:

  • Reduce lag: Lighten the load on the system, helping you play games more smoothly, even on weaker devices.
  • Remove particle: Eliminate particle effects to enhance performance.
  • Beautiful UI: The interface is designed in the most beautiful and lively way.
